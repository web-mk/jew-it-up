With Handmade Matzah (no dough required)

This Passover, Jew it up with a free box of 3 premium handmade matzahs – compliments of Chabad-Lubavitch.

This matzah isn’t just any cracker. It reconnects us to our ancestors' hurried escape from Egypt – carrying with it the spiritual energy of faith, freedom, hope and redemption.

So be sure to eat THIS matzah at the Seder! (Just try to keep the crumbs under control.)
