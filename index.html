There’s a lot more to Purim than “Pour ‘em.”

Sure, you’ll pour a few L’Chaims. And make lots of noise. And wear silly masks. But with antisemitism skyrocketing, the story of Purim is more relevant than ever. So don’t miss Purim at Chabad-Lubavitch, where we’ll read the Megillah, blot out Haman’s name and learn how one woman’s courage saved an entire nation.
