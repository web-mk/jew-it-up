There’s a lot
more to Purim
than “Pour ‘em.”

Sure, you’ll pour a few L’Chaims. And make lots of noise. And wear silly masks. But with antisemitism skyrocketing, the story of Purim is more relevant than ever. So this Purim, Jew It up at Chabad-Lubavitch, where we’ll read the Megillah, learn how one woman’s courage saved an entire nation, and celebrate this beautiful festival with greater joy, meaning, and spirit!
